About Me

My name is Elijah Porter. I am 20 years old and a Junior in the MSU Computer Science program. I got interested in programming when I first started elementary school by reading books on HTML and web development at the library.

Here are a few things that I like:

  • Concrete
  • Basements
  • Water

It's cool because all of those are related. You use water to mix concrete, you use concrete to make basements, and you don't want water collecting in your basement. I used to work as a laborer for a concrete contractor, if you couldn't tell.
